About us

Itech Academia is an outcome of two Python-loving friends exploring their interests in web development. Despite owing their origin to different engineering streams, it was the zeal for learning that got them working together in this project. Itech Academia took around four weeks to arrive at a structure and another two weeks for testing and hosting. They extend their utmost gratitude to lockdown for letting them invest time in developing this website. Being engineering students in PSG Institute of Technology and Applied Research themselves, they felt the need to have structured notes, technological updates, and GPA calculators all under one roof and hence this project. Happy learning!
